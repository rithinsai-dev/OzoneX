How You Can Help Protect the Ozone Layer

Avoid CFC Products
Avoid using products containing harmful chemicals like chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs).
Maintain Refrigeration Equipment
Ensure your air conditioning and refrigeration equipment is well-maintained to prevent refrigerant leaks.
Use Eco-Friendly Products
Use eco-friendly household cleaning products to reduce chemical pollution.
Reduce Carbon Emissions
Minimize the use of vehicles by walking, biking, or using public transport to reduce carbon emissions.
Support Ozone Policies
Promote and support policies that aim to reduce ozone-depleting substances.
Use Energy Efficient Devices
Opt for energy-efficient appliances and lighting to decrease energy consumption.
